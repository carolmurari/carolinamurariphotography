Family Portraits

Family is the most important part of my life. As a mother, wife, sister, and daughter I know in my heart how important it is to create memories and celebrate life with the people we love. So, to have a beautiful family portrait showing your child's unique laugh, how they play and have fun together at a particular moment in time is priceless. That is my aim, to give you amazing photos to return to...

Bumps and Newborns

Pregnancy is so challenging and so beautiful. The 40 weeks are long and short at the same time. It's long because you can't wait to meet the new member of your family and short in so far as you are never too prepared for what is ahead. I love to photograph bumps. It's a great moment in a family's life. It's exciting, it's new, or even when it is not your first or second baby, it still is exciting. And the icing in the cake it is, after capturing your beautiful bump, to be able to photograph your baby when he comes to the world.

Weddings

Only you know long it took to organize your wedding day how much planning was involved and how special you want that day to be but the photographs will bring back great memories of that day. It will show you hilarious and details that you don't want to forget. It will put a smile on your face and an emotional tear in your eye every time you see how amazing you both looked, or how you looked at each other. That is what I try to do at every wedding I photograph.
